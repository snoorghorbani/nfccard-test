HiHello is a digital business card and team management platform. Its features are designed to serve both **individual users** and **enterprise/business clients**. Below is a structured overview.

---

## **1. Features for Regular Users / Individuals**
- **Digital Business Card**
  - Create and manage a single digital card.
  - Customizable fields: name, title, company, photo, links.
  - Multiple profiles/cards (personal, work, event).
- **Sharing Options**
  - QR code, NFC, link, text, email, Apple/Google Wallet.
- **Contact Management**
  - Download vCard / save to contacts.
  - Basic contact organization.
- **Analytics**
  - Track card views and shares.
- **Custom Content**
  - Add resume, portfolio links, or embedded video (optional).
- **Networking**
  - Social link previews, instant actions (call, email, visit website).

---

## **2. Features for Enterprise / Business Users**
- **Team & Employee Management**
  - Invite/create employee accounts.
  - Role-based permissions (Owner/Admin/Member/Employee).
  - Sub-teams and department organization.
  - Bulk onboarding/offboarding and provisioning.
- **Branding & Templates**
  - Company-wide templates with locked fields.
  - Branded cards for distributed teams.
  - Virtual backgrounds for video meetings.
- **Lead Capture & CRM Integration**
  - Custom lead capture flows embedded in cards.
  - Event lead retrieval and card scanning at scale.
  - Direct sync to CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot, Microsoft Dynamics).
- **Analytics & Insights**
  - Aggregated dashboards for team performance.
  - Leaderboards, engagement trends, exportable reports.
- **Email Signature Management**
  - Centralized automated signature manager.
  - Enforce brand consistency across all employees.
- **Security & Compliance**
  - Single Sign-On (SSO), directory sync (Okta, Entra ID, Workday).
  - SOC2 Type II certified, GDPR / CCPA compliance.
  - Enterprise encryption and access controls.
- **Advanced Integrations & Automation**
  - Marketing automation (Mailchimp, Marketo), Zapier workflows.
  - Bulk card creation and dynamic updates.
